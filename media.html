BBC, UNESCO & International Media

Dr. Amara has provided expert commentary and research contributions to BBC, UNESCO, and various international media outlets on topics including:

  • Sport and multiculturalism debates in Europe
  • Football and political change in North Africa (Arab Spring, Hirak)
  • Islamic perspectives on sport and physical activity
  • Qatar's sport mega-event strategy and Gulf sport governance
  • African football identities and the World Cup
